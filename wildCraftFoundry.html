WildCraft Foundry
| e-commerce platform for 3D models

What I Did

  • Created an e-commerce platform for 3D models for a 3D artist
  • Implementation in Shopify for secure payment
  • The Challenge: The artist needed a way to turn casual visitors into dedicated Patreon supporters. The site had to bridge two very different worlds: the medieval fantasy of Dungeons & Dragons and the high-tech of 3D modeling and printing.
  • The Solution: I designed a digital space where 'Tech meets Medieval’. By highlighting the artist's dedication and personality through custom layouts, the site feels like a personal workshop rather than just a store, making users more likely to donate.
