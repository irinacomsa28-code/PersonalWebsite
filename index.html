About Me

While my master's degree in media informatics provides a good foundation in computer science, I thrive at the intersection of creative design and technical implementation.

My experience at Usaneers offered me hands-on experience of UX and programming for XR environments, while my time at TUDesign student club allowed me to sharpen my design thinking in a collaborative setting.

I am now seeking a collaborative environment, where I can learn from experts. I am particularly drawn to high-stakes sectors such as HealthTech, Security, and Fintech—areas where technology meets genuine human impact. Regarding the current AI wave, I am particularly interested in the ethics and security behind it. I view AI as a tool meant to support humans, rather than replace the essential human touch.
