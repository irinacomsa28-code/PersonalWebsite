Serious Game for Social Impact
| bachelor thesis

What I Did

  • Conceptualized & designed a serious game to mitigate gender stereotypes in children
  • Expert-led: designed based on research and a critical design session with researchers in Child-Computer Interaction
  • Implemented the game in Unity (C#)
  • User-centered design: conducted two user sessions and redesigned the game’s second half based on the results from the first session
  • Validated game’s effectiveness via direct interaction and a reflection coding scheme

Keywords

  • Child-Computer Interaction
  • Reflective Design
  • Critical Design
  • Serious Games

People Involved

  • Prof. Dr. Albrecht Schmidt
  • Prof. Dr. Monica Landoni
  • PhD student Irene Zanardi
  • four researchers in CCI
  • eight primary-school children
